Welcome to WASend

Sometimes you need to send "WhatsApp®" phone numbers that you don't have in your contacts. You may see an advertisement, or an old friend gave it to you on a piece of paper. You must save this number in your contacts and then you can write the text you need.

Now you don't need to save temporary contacts to chat! WASend is a tool that helps you put the number that WhatsApp writes and opens ready for your conversation.

WASend, Send WhatsApp messages to phone numbers that you do not have in your contacts.
